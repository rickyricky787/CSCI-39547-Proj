Mission Statement: our aim is to...

  • Improve the health of the community by developing a sustainable local food system to increase the accessibility of healthy food options, such as fruits and vegetables, (particularly for lower-income families and individuals) to the community.
  • Empower the community by providing education in the form of nutrition and food preparation seminars
  • Bring the diverse people of the community together

About our Garden:

Open every Saturday from 9:00am-3:00pm!

Participants over the age of 13 are more than welcome to join us during work hours; adults must accompany younger children.

Volunteer at the Garden!

Join our Urban Youth Program! A 3-month internship where volunters receive an in-depth and hands-on experience in environmental science, agriculture, and green technology. Those who are interested will be allowed to work on our own personal acre farm and greenhouse from which any fruit/vegetable produced will be supplied to the applicant's own farm stand! We will be hosting weekly workshops related to a variety of topics ranging from the environmental effects of pesticide-use to industrial farming and deforestation.
